Consensus-Based Framework of Core Clinical Activities
Final Criteria Set (n = 40)

Study Overview

This framework presents the final consensus-based set of 40 clinical activities identified through a two-round modified e-Delphi study involving 39 clinical pharmacists from 13 European countries. The aim was to define the core clinical activities for clinical pharmacists working in mental health across Europe.

Methodology Summary

  • Design: Modified electronic Delphi (e‑Delphi)
  • Rounds: Two
  • Panel: 39 experts across 13 countries
  • Eligibility: ≥3 years of psychiatric clinical pharmacy experience
  • Scale: 4‑point Likert
  • Consensus: ≥80% agreement, Median ≥3, IQR ≤1
